The MCP Play: We don't fight their security features; we extend them. Opsera is the "Enterprise Plugin" they call via MCP to move code from their garden to the customer's private cloud (AWS/GCP).

Pure AI IDEs (Cursor/Claude)

Amazing creation tools but lack a native production path or deep governance layers.

Counter: Opsera completes them via MCP. We are the infrastructure they are missing.

The Strategy: Distribution over Destination

We do not try to out-vibe them on creation by building another walled garden. We win by distributing our MCP-native infrastructure directly into their tools, making them safe for the Fortune 500. They provide the harness; we distribute the engine.
